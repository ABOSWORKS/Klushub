# Growth Analytics

## Use when
- The task asks for KPI frameworks, conversion analysis, or growth bottleneck diagnosis.
- The task needs metric trees for customer and contractor funnels.

## Workflow
1. Build metric tree from north star to actionable drivers.
2. Split by key cohorts (region, category, acquisition source, contractor tier).
3. Identify bottlenecks with estimated impact.
4. Prioritize interventions by impact x effort x confidence.

## Output format
- `North star and drivers`
- `Funnel by stage`
- `Cohort signals`
- `Top 3 interventions`

## Standards
- Always include both demand-side and supply-side metrics.
- Add data quality assumptions and uncertainty notes.
