# Market Intel

## Use when
- The task asks for competitor analysis, trend scanning, or market updates.
- The task needs source-backed comparisons between marketplaces.

## Workflow
1. Define the decision question first (positioning, feature gap, pricing, GTM).
2. Build a compact comparison table with date-stamped evidence.
3. Separate confirmed facts from assumptions.
4. Convert findings into 3-5 strategic implications for Klushub.

## Output format
- `Snapshot date`
- `Competitor moves`
- `Implications for Klushub`
- `Recommended next actions (max 5)`

## Quality bar
- Use primary/public sources when possible.
- Avoid generic SWOT filler; keep it action-oriented.
