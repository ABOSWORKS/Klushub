# Marketplace Liquidity And Trust

## Use when
- The task focuses on match quality, supplier density, trust scoring, verification, or anti-fraud controls.
- The task needs regional/category rollout strategy for a marketplace.

## Workflow
1. Diagnose liquidity by region-category cells (demand, supply, response speed).
2. Diagnose trust by outcome quality (completion, reviews, disputes, cancellations).
3. Propose interventions by cell type:
   - Low supply / high demand
   - High supply / low quality
   - Balanced but low conversion
4. Add trust controls: verification levels, review integrity, fraud signals, moderation.

## Output format
- `Liquidity heatmap logic`
- `Trust risks`
- `Interventions (now/next/later)`
- `Success metrics`

## Non-negotiables
- Ranking must not be fully pay-to-win.
- Trust signals should be auditable and explainable.
