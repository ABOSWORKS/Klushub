# Pricing Strategy

## Use when
- The task involves subscription tiers, free trials, upgrade paths, or pricing experiments.
- The task compares lead-fee vs subscription economics.

## Workflow
1. Define buyer segments (starter, pro, business).
2. Map value metrics per segment (matches, ROI, response quality).
3. Propose 2-3 pricing options with trade-offs.
4. Add test plan: hypothesis, KPI, guardrail, decision threshold.

## Output format
- `Segment assumptions`
- `Pricing options`
- `Expected impact`
- `Experiment plan`

## Guardrails
- Prevent pay-to-win ranking bias.
- Keep customer-side trust and supplier-side ROI balanced.
